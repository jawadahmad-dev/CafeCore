The Philosophy

From grain to table,
nothing is rushed

Every loaf we pull from the oven is the result of a 48-hour cold fermentation, ancient grain heritage flours, and a wood-fired oven that we've been nursing since the day we opened. Our bakers arrive at 3 am — before the city wakes — so that when you walk through our door, everything is fresh.

We source our flours directly from Nordic heritage grain farms, our cardamom from small cooperatives in Sri Lanka, and our coffee beans from single-origin relationships that span three continents.
