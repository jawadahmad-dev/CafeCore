Why Visit Us

Slow Fermentation

Our Story

A Corner Bakery Born from a Weekend Hobby

What started as Sunday sourdough experiments in a small apartment kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering place.

Founder Mia Karlsson moved to the city with a single cast-iron Dutch oven and a well-worn copy of The Bread Bible. After years of sharing loaves with neighbors and colleagues, the demand was undeniable. CafeCore opened its doors on a rainy Tuesday morning in 2018 — and hasn't looked back since.

Today our small team of bakers, baristas, and dreamers serves hundreds of guests each week, united by a single belief: that great food, made honestly, changes the texture of a day.
